Dieter Rams'

Ten Principles of Good Design

  • 1

    Good design is innovative

    Design should keep up with trends and offer new and exciting ideas.

    • NYTimes.com Coutesy of MOMA

  • 2

    Good design makes a product useful

    Something created should have a reason for why it was created, perhaps it exists to solve a problem or communicate a message clearly

    • NYTimes.com Courtesy of Braun

  • 3

    Good design is aesthetic

    Not only should design be useful but it should also be pleasing to the eye.

  • 4

    Good design makes a product understandable

    People that are not in the field of design should be able to use the product created, and have an understanding of what the designer is trying to acheive.

  • 5

    Good design is honest

    A good designer does not copy other people's work and comes up with their own ideas, they should have good intentions.

  • 6

    Good design is unobtrusive

    A designer should focus on an item's function rather than ornamentation. It is better to have a simple design rather than one full of many distracting small details.

  • 7

    Good design is long-lasting

    Things designed should be relevant to today and stay relevant in the future.

    • NYTimes.com Courtesy of Braun

  • 8

    Good design is thorough down to the last detail

    Even though it is not possible to make something completely perfect all of the time, designers should strive to be as precise as possible when working on all aspects of a design.

  • 9

    Good design is environmentally friendly

    Designers should be resourceful and not be wasteful when it comes to the materials and tools they use, because it is important to keep nature in mind.

  • 10

    Good design is as little design as possible

    Even though designers should try to follow principles of design, it is also acceptable to take a step back and be a little more free when designing.

    • vitsoe.com Courtesy of Braun
